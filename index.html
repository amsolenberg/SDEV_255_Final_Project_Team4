Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get to Rigel 4?

At light speed, approximately 25 years, at Warp 5 approximately 5 years, at Warp 10 approximately 2.5 years.

What language is spoken on Rigel 4?

By coincidence, Rigellian and English are exactly the same.

How much does attendance cost?

The cost is free to residents of Earth.
